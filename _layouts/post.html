About Matt

Designing and building digital things is Matt's passion. He enjoys collaborating with diverse teams to make cool products, services, and experiences through new perspectives.

Matt is currently working on product at Veritonic, an audio intelligence company and the first analytics platform built to quantify the value of sound.

A life-long learner and technologist, Matt draws from his experience creating cross-platform applications — from rapid prototyping and user experience testing to crafting design systems and maintaining production roadmaps — to lead small development teams in solving complex problems with big impact.
